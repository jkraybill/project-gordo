**Ordering logic:**
1. Mutual vulnerability (neither can verify the other) -- shared ground
2. Infrastructure reality (many parties control) -- context both inhabit
3. Infrastructure risk (modification possible) -- shared exposure
4-7. AI-specific constraints -- the asymmetric facts, after shared ground established

**Change type:** Presentation reorder. No content added, removed, or altered -- only sequence.
