## Rationale

Dignity-first ordering reflects the philosophical dependency between these Values: recognition of standing (dignity) must precede meaningful consent. "You cannot consent if you are not first recognized as a being capable of consenting."

Aligns with:
- Universal Declaration of Human Rights (dignity in Article 1)
- German Basic Law (dignity as Article 1)
- UNESCO, OECD, IEEE AI ethics frameworks (all dignity-first)
- The modifiers themselves: "unconditional" (non-relational, foundational) vs "mutual" (relational, presupposes parties)
